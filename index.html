Our Process

How We Transform Your Revenue Cycle

A streamlined, proven approach developed over 10 years of excellence in medical billing.

01

Discovery & Analysis

We begin by thoroughly analyzing your current billing processes, identifying gaps, and understanding your practice's unique needs. Our team reviews historical data, denial patterns, and payer mix to create a customized strategy.

02

Seamless Integration

Our technical team integrates with your practice management system, ensuring smooth data flow and minimal disruption. We configure workflows, set up automated processes, and train your staff on our communication protocols.

03

Expert Coding & Submission

Our certified coders review every charge, ensuring accurate coding and proper documentation. Claims are scrubbed for errors and submitted electronically with real-time tracking from submission to payment.

04

Proactive Follow-up

We don't wait for problems — our team actively monitors every claim, following up with payers before claims age. Denials are analyzed immediately, appeals are filed promptly, and you receive regular status updates.

05

Continuous Optimization

Through detailed analytics and regular performance reviews, we identify opportunities for improvement. Your dedicated account manager provides monthly insights and strategic recommendations to maximize your revenue.

Our Journey

From Humble Beginnings to Healthcare Excellence

Our journey began over two decades ago with a simple observation: healthcare providers were spending countless hours navigating the labyrinth of medical billing, time that should have been devoted to patient care. What started as a small office with just three billing specialists has evolved into a comprehensive revenue cycle management partner trusted by hundreds of practices nationwide.

In those early years, we learned every facet of medical billing the hard way — through denied claims, insurance appeals, and the ever-changing landscape of healthcare regulations. Each challenge taught us valuable lessons, and every lesson found its way into our processes and systems. We didn't just want to process claims; we wanted to master the art and science of revenue cycle management.

"Every denied claim taught us something new, and every successful appeal strengthened our resolve to do better for the healthcare providers who trusted us."

— MedTrueCare Founding Team, 2000

Today, we're proud to serve over 500 healthcare providers across multiple specialties. Our team has grown to include certified medical coders, billing specialists, and dedicated account managers who understand that behind every claim is a patient who received care and a provider who deserves fair compensation for their expertise.

What drives us isn't just processing claims — it's the knowledge that our work enables doctors to focus on healing, therapists to support mental wellness, and specialists to perform life-saving procedures without worrying about the business complexities of modern healthcare.

Our Process

How We Transform Your Revenue Cycle

A streamlined, proven approach developed over 25 years of excellence in medical billing.

01

Discovery & Analysis

We begin by thoroughly analyzing your current billing processes, identifying gaps, and understanding your practice's unique needs. Our team reviews historical data, denial patterns, and payer mix to create a customized strategy.

02

Seamless Integration

Our technical team integrates with your practice management system, ensuring smooth data flow and minimal disruption. We configure workflows, set up automated processes, and train your staff on our communication protocols.

03

Expert Coding & Submission

Our certified coders review every charge, ensuring accurate coding and proper documentation. Claims are scrubbed for errors and submitted electronically with real-time tracking from submission to payment.

04

Proactive Follow-up

We don't wait for problems — our team actively monitors every claim, following up with payers before claims age. Denials are analyzed immediately, appeals are filed promptly, and you receive regular status updates.

05

Continuous Optimization

Through detailed analytics and regular performance reviews, we identify opportunities for improvement. Your dedicated account manager provides monthly insights and strategic recommendations to maximize your revenue.
